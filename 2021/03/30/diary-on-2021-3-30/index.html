发表于 2021-03-30 分类于 日记类
一些伤心的事情

Today,I’m not as happy as others from the afternoon.
I want to say something to you.

main

I love you since the first winter vacation of the middle school.To nowadays,I love you for about 1.5 years.I can tell you that somebody surprised that I will give my love to you.However,I disagree with that.
During the online class,I chat with you amost everynight.That time,I think the most meaningful things is to do the things you want.
But I know that you hate me like the first person I love at middle school because some reason I don’t know after online classes,but I still love you.”My sweet hurt,I’m waiting for you in Keketuo Sea,but they say that you got married to Ili.”That song made me cry at a sad night,because it’s touching for me.Yeah,I still don’t give up.
I know,I’m not as handsome as him(in other class,and I don’t know the name).Maybe,my study isn’t as great as him.So now,I think the best choice is to respect you.I think I won’t love others during middle school.
